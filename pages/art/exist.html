To Love We Just Need to Exist

Two channel video projection, 13 minute 57 second loop

2021

In this installation, I scrounged through countless hours of archival footage from the 1900s to the 2000s. In my search, I stitched together clips of birds flying in the air aligned along one path. I projected this sequence above on an air vent. In a sink filled with water beneath it, I projected water footage found from an archival documentary of the US Navy in the Vietnam War.
